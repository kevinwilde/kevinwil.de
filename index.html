Experience

This past summer I worked as a software engineering intern at Coilcraft, Inc. During my time there, I fully developed (both front and back end) four web applications using the ASP.NET framework. These applications will be used at at least five of Coilcraft's different locations and can be entirely maintained within the application by an application admin. This will allow Coilcraft to continually change and update the applications without ever having to modify the code.

A large part of my experience also comes from projects that I have worked on in my free time or for classes. You can see some examples of my projects below.

My Projects

Analysis of EPL Passing Statistics

This was a personal project that I worked on in order to analyze the performance of soccer players in the English Premier League. I used k-means clustering to try to group players with similar passing styles into clusters and also mainpulated the data in several scatter plots to look at both individual players and overall trends.

Facial Recognition Simulator

This was a really cool project that I did using MATLAB. The image here is an example of the output from one section of the simulator. The simulator was able to look at a corrupted image, reconstruct it, and correctly identify the image from the database that came from.

Web Communication Simulator
